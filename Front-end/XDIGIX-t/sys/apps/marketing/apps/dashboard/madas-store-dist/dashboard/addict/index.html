About Addict

At Addict, we believe in the beauty of handmade craftsmanship. Each piece in our collection is carefully created by skilled artisans who pour their passion and creativity into every stitch, every detail, and every unique design.

Our mission is to bring you one-of-a-kind treasures that not only look beautiful but also carry the warmth and story of the hands that made them. From crocheted bags to handcrafted accessories, every item is a work of art.
